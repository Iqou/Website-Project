Denji

Denji, initially leading a bleak and mundane life, experiences a dramatic turn of events after his debt-ridden father passes away. To settle his father's debts, Denji takes on dangerous and odd jobs while accompanied by his loyal devil pet, Pochita. However, tragedy strikes when Denji loses his life in a demon attack, only to be revived by Pochita merging with him and transforming him into Chainsaw Man.

Chainsaw Man possesses incredible strength and abilities, with chainsaw blades protruding from various parts of his body. As Denji becomes an official devil hunter, he navigates the complex and perilous world of devil hunting, facing powerful adversaries and uncovering dark secrets.
